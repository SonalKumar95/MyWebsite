ABOUT

I, Sonal Kumar, received the M.Tech. degree in Computer Science and Engineering with a gold medal from the National Institute of Technology Patna and a B.E. degree in Information Science and Engineering from VTU University. Currently, I am actively engaged in pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IIT Guwahati).

RESEARCH AREA

My current research focuses on exploring self-supervised learning-based approaches for Computer Vision applications like Image Classification, Semantic Segmentation, Object Discovery, Medical Image Synthesis, Report Generation, etc. I have also explored the applications of blockchain technology in the IoT domain during M.Tech research.

PROJECTS

  • IPCL, an iterative pseudo-supervised contrastive learning framework, enhancing feature representation for unsupervised image classification tasks. [GitHub]
  • DatUS , a data-driven framework for unsupervised semantic segmentation utilizing a self-supervised vision transformer without labels.[GitHub]
  • GraPix, a novel graph-based unsupervised pixel clustering technique, achieving +5\% improvement over existing SOTA methods.[GitHub]
  • River-GEM, a very first approach to generate and enhanced muddy-water images form river environments.[GitHub]
  • Ongoing works focus on multi-model learning, unsupervised object discovery, underwater image generation with diffusion model, medical report generation, and unsupervised few-shot learning.[GitHub]

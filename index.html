ABOUT

I, Sonal Kumar, received the M.Tech. degree in Computer Science and Engineering with a gold medal from NIT Patna and a B.E. degree from VTU. I am pursuing a Ph.D. at IIT Guwahati in Computer Science and Engineering.

RESEARCH AREA

My research focuses on self-supervised learning for computer vision applications like image classification, semantic segmentation, object discovery, medical image synthesis, and more. Earlier work includes blockchain applications in IoT.

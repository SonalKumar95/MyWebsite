About Me

I, Sonal Kumar, received the M.Tech. degree in Computer Science and Engineering with a gold medal from the National Institute of Technology Patna and a B.E. degree in Information Science and Engineering from VTU University. Currently, I am actively engaged in pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati.

Research Area

My research focuses on exploring self-supervised learning-based approaches for Computer Vision applications like Image Classification, Semantic Segmentation, Object Discovery, Medical Image Synthesis, and Report Generation etc.
